About RescueConnect

Learn about our mission, vision, and the amazing team behind our life-saving platform

Our Mission

To leverage technology for saving lives during disasters by providing timely alerts, efficient coordination between victims and rescuers, and comprehensive emergency management tools that work even without internet connectivity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no life is lost due to lack of timely information or coordination during disasters, where technology serves as a bridge between those in need and those who can help.

Our Story

RescueConnect was founded in 2020 after our founders witnessed the devastating impact of natural disasters and the communication gaps that hindered rescue efforts. We realized that technology could play a crucial role in saving lives if designed specifically for emergency scenarios.

Today, RescueConnect is used in over 30 countries by emergency services, NGOs, and individuals alike. Our platform has been instrumental in saving thousands of lives during earthquakes, floods, hurricanes, and other disasters.

SOS Emergency System

Instant emergency alerts with multiple activation methods even without internet connection

Emergency Assistance When You Need It Most

Our SOS system is designed to work in the most challenging conditions. Whether you're injured, trapped, or in danger, RescueConnect provides multiple ways to call for help.

With offline functionality, your emergency signals can be sent even without cellular service using our mesh network technology.

Demo SOS Features

How to Activate SOS

1

Button Press

Press and hold the emergency button for 3 seconds on the main screen

2

Voice Command

Say "Emergency" or "Help me" three times to activate voice SOS

3

Gesture Control

Shake your device vigorously for 5 seconds to trigger emergency mode

4

Automated Detection

Let our AI detect accidents based on sound patterns and impact sensors

What Happens When You Activate SOS

When emergency mode is activated, RescueConnect immediately:

  • Sends your precise location to emergency contacts and services
  • Shares your medical information with first responders
  • Activates your phone's microphone to capture ambient sounds
  • Attempts to establish mesh network connections with nearby devices
  • Continues sending updates until help arrives or you cancel the alert
Configure Emergency Contacts

Instant Alert System

Our SOS system sends immediate alerts to emergency services, nearby volunteers, and your designated contacts with your precise location and vital information.

Voice Activation

When you can't press buttons, use voice commands like "Emergency" or "Help me" to activate the SOS system hands-free.

Gesture Control

Shake your device vigorously for 5 seconds to trigger emergency mode when you're unable to speak or press buttons.

Offline Functionality

Even without cellular service, our mesh network technology allows your SOS signals to reach help through nearby devices.
